Instructions
  1. Choose a fresh stem of a plant (Amaranthus).
  2. Take a small portion of stem.
  3. Cut the stem transversely.
  4. Shift these sections with the help of a brush in or watch glass containing water.
  5. Transfer the stem section onto a glass microscope slide.
  6. Add 2- 3 drops solution of safranin for 2-5 minutes.
  7. Rinse the sections briefly in distilled water.
  8. Add fast green for a few seconds to a minute.
  9. Rinse the section in distilled water.
  10. Add a drop of water.
  11. Place a coverslip over the stem section.
  12. Place the prepared clean slide on the stage of the microscope.
  13. Start with the magnification objective lens.
  14. You will see a figure like this of the cross section of the stem.
