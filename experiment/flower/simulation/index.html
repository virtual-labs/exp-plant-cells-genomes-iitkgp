Instructions for flower
  1. Select green leaf from the dropdrop box.
  2. Then Take a small section of leaf area between veins and place it in a cork.
  3. Cut a small section of the leaf
  4. Shift these sections with the help of a brush in or watch glass containing water.
  5. Transfer the leaf section onto a glass microscope slide.
  6. Add a drop of water to the leaf
  7. place a coverslip over the leaf section
  8. Place the prepared clean slide on the stage of the microscope
  9. Start with the magnification objective lens
  10. You will see a figure like this of the cross section of the leaf
